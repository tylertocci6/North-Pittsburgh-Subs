Career Opportunities

Available Positions:

  • Part-time Employee: This is available for anyone that wants to work less than 30 hours a week and has no previous experience.
  • Shift Leaders: Previous experience is preferred. You will be in charge of opening and closing the store.
  • Assistant Managers: This position requires that you work full-time and have previous experience. You will be keeping track of inventory and training new employees.
  • Managers: This position requires that you work full-time and have previous experience. You will be creating the employee schedule and placing produce orders.
  • Regional Managers: Previous managing experience is required. This position invloves visting all store locations and ensuring they are running properly.

How to Join:

Interviews can be scheduled in person at any store or you can setup an interview by submitting your informationhere.
