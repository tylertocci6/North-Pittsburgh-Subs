Our Story

North Pittsburgh Subs was founded in 2022 by Tyler Tocci with a goal to make the best subs in Pennsylvania. Everything about our business is high quality. Our MSA Grade top round beef are trimmed and cooked right in the store. Our meats and cheeses are all top-quality premium brands. Our bread is fresh-baked each day on the premises and our subs are prepared right in front of you.
